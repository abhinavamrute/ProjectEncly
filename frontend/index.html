How We Protect You

No IP logging. Not even temporarily.

We've designed our network architecture to never see or store your IP address. Unlike other services that claim "we only log IPs temporarily," we don't have the capability to log them at all.

Messages never touch a server — not even encrypted

Using advanced peer-to-peer encryption protocols, your messages are routed directly between users without passing through our infrastructure. We literally can't see your communications.

Screenshot blocker tech

Our proprietary client-side technology prevents screenshots and screen recordings of sensitive conversations. The content only exists in your memory.

IP hopping via VPN rotation

Even if someone tries to monitor your network traffic, our system automatically rotates through multiple VPN endpoints to obscure your digital footprint.

Metadata stripping and anti-AI data protections

We remove all metadata from files and messages, and employ techniques to prevent AI analysis of content patterns that could deanonymize users.

Full erase = zero trace

When you delete content, it's permanently erased with military-grade wiping algorithms. No fragments remain in memory, storage, or backups.
